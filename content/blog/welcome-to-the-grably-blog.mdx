Running same-day cannabis delivery in British Columbia means living inside the LCRB's recordkeeping rules — driver identity on every order, age verification captured at the door, proof of delivery, and a manifest you can produce on demand. We built Grably because the gap between "we deliver" and "we can prove every delivery" is where retailers get fined.

This blog is where we'll write about what that actually looks like day to day. Expect practical breakdowns of the delivery requirements inspectors check for, notes on the mistakes we see most often, and short explainers you can hand to a new driver on their first shift.

We'll also use this space for product updates. When we ship something that changes how you dispatch, verify age, or pull an audit-ready log, we'll explain what it does and why it matters — no jargon, no fluff.

If there's a compliance question you keep running into, tell us. The best posts here will come from the real problems BC retailers are trying to solve, and we'd rather write about those than guess. Reach the team any time at hello@grably.ca.
